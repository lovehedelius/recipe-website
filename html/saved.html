Saved Recipes

More functionality coming soon!

Thought For Food is developed as part of the course Dynamiska Webbapplikationer at Uppsala University. Because of the restrictions on the project, the contents of this page can't be added dynamically with JavaScript. Your saved recipes can therefore not be displayed here. Instead, an example of how the page might look when fully implemented is shown below, with a static list of recipes rather than your actual saved ones. In the fully functional version, the recipe names will be clickable and take you to their detailed page. It will also be possible to click the heart shaped buttons to unsave recipes you no longer want saved, which will make them not appear the next time the page is opened.
